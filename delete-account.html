deletion request

You have the right to delete your SoleMates account and all associated personal data at any time. Choose the method that works best for you below.

Fastest — instant
Delete directly in the app
If you have the SoleMates app installed, you can delete your account and all data instantly from within the app.
1
Open SoleMates on your device
2
Tap the Account tab (last tab in the bottom bar)
3
Scroll to Danger zone → tap Delete my account
4
Type DELETE to confirm → tap Delete my account
This will permanently delete:
  • Your profile and Google sign-in link
  • Your entire training plan and schedule
  • All logged workouts, pace and heart rate data
  • Your progress history and personal bests
Via email — within 30 days
Submit a deletion request
Don't have the app? Submit a request below and we'll delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.
✓

Request received

Your deletion request has been sent to privacy@solemates.app. We will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days and send you a confirmation email.

Direct email
Email us directly
You can also email us directly at any time with a deletion request.
1
Email privacy@solemates.app
2
Subject: Account Deletion Request
3
Include your name and the Gmail address linked to your account

What gets deleted: your profile, Google sign-in link, training plan, all workout logs, progress data, and personal bests.

What we retain: anonymised, non-identifiable aggregate statistics only (no personal data).

Retention period: all personal data is purged within 30 days of a confirmed request.
