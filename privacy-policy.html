Section 4

Data sharing & third parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Google Firebase — authentication and database hosting (EU region). Bound by Google's Data Processing Agreement.
  • Google Analytics for Firebase — anonymised, aggregated usage statistics only. No personal data.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order or government authority.
  • Business transfer — if SoleMates is acquired, you will be notified 30 days before your data transfers to any new owner.
No advertising networks. No data brokers. No marketing partners. We do not build advertising profiles or share personal data with any commercial third party.
Section 5

Data storage & security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers located in the European Union (Belgium, eu-west1), ensuring compliance with GDPR data residency requirements.

We implement the following security measures:

  • All data encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher
  • All data encrypted at rest using AES-256
  • Authentication handled entirely by Google OAuth 2.0 — we never store passwords
  • Access to production data restricted to authorised personnel only
  • Regular security reviews and dependency auditing

Section 8

Children's privacy

SoleMates is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at privacy@solemates.app and we will delete it promptly.

Section 9

Google Sign-In

When you sign in with Google, you authorise SoleMates to access your basic profile (name, email, profile photo) as shown in the Google OAuth consent screen. We do not receive or store your Google password.

You can revoke SoleMates' access to your Google account at any time at myaccount.google.com/permissions. Revoking access signs you out but does not delete your SoleMates data — use the in-app delete account feature for full data removal.

Section 10

Push notifications

SoleMates may send push notifications about your training schedule and goal milestones. You can opt in during onboarding and opt out at any time via your device's notification settings or in the SoleMates Account tab.

We never use push notifications for advertising or send unsolicited marketing messages.

Section 11

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • Send an in-app notification at least 30 days before changes take effect
  • Ask for renewed consent where required by law

Continued use of SoleMates after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
